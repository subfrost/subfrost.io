- **Step 1: Mining Pool Partnership**
    - Existing partnerships with mining pools within project network
    - Targeting at least 1 pool initially, eventually all major pools
    - Pools mint coinbase rewards directly into SUBFROST native NFTs
- **Step 2: Futures NFT Creation**
    - SUBFROST native NFT created atomically with coinbase transaction
    - Entire coinbase reward flows into frBTC reserve atomically
    - NFT represents claim on locked coinbase output
    - NFT can be burned after 30 confirmations to release frBTC
- **Step 3: Optimization & Liquidity**
    - Coinbase lock period (100 blocks) is outdated from Satoshi-era consensus
    - Modern mining pool centralization enables optimization through partnerships
    - Early burn (prior to 100 block maturity) enables futures trading
    - Creates liquid trading paths natively on SUBFROST
    - Miners can hedge new BTC and improve profits with additional controls
    - Traders pay premium for early liquidity, discount at maturity
    - dxBTC captures upside from this optimization
